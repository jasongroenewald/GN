About the Programme

GrowNation is a yearly programme that aims  to improve the lives of rural farmers with the opportunity to use their lands for commercial farming. We do this by providing training, raw materials, resources, constant monitoring and advice and guaranteed purchase of their full harvest at the end of the growing season.

The agricultural sector in South Africa employs more than 10%  of the country’s population. Any innovation ensuring higher  yields while keeping production costs low will have  wide-ranging impact.

Challenge Journey

  • 22 - 28 July

    Submit Application

    Submit your application to join our programme for 2019

  • 29 July - 15 September

    Multi-Level Selection Process

    Each application will go through a selection process, including assessments of their land

  • 16 - 29 September

    Final Selection

    The farms that make the shortlist will receive a contract for the 2019/2020 farming season to provide sorghum

  • 30 Sept - 3 Nov

    Land Preparation

    Together with our project coordinators, we will assist you  in getting your lands reading  for planting

  • 18 Nov 2019 - 27 July 2020

    Planting, Harvesting & Final Delivery

    Together with our project coordinators, we will monitor  and assist through the the whole journey from seed to harvest, ending with the final delivery  and payment.
