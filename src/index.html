About the Programme

GrowNation is a yearly programme that aims  to improve the lives of rural farmers with the opportunity to use their lands for commercial farming. We do this by providing training, raw materials, resources, constant monitoring and advice and guaranteed purchase of their full harvest at the end of the growing season.

The agricultural sector in South Africa employs more than 10%  of the country’s population. Any innovation ensuring higher  yields while keeping production costs low will have  wide-ranging impact.
